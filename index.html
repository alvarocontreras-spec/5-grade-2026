My Daily Routine

A2 Writing Assessment - 5th Year

🎯 Goal: Describe your routine using Present Simple and connectors.
📝 Instructions:
  • Write 5–6 sentences.
  • Range: 40 - 60 words.
  • Use: and, also, moreover, in addition, but, because.
🧱 Vocabulary Box:
Morning, afternoon, night, lunch, school, home, study, watch TV, happy, fun.
📌 Model (Absurd Example - Do not copy!):
I wake up at 3:00 am and I go to Mars in the morning. I eat blue screws for lunch, also in the afternoon, I play with my pet dragon. Moreover, I like dragons because they breathe fire. Finally, I go to bed at noon.
